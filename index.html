"Bart" Simpson

A.K.A. El Barto and Bartman, is the mischievous, rebellious, misunderstood, and "potentially dangerous" eldest child of Homer and Marge Simpson, and the brother of Lisa and Maggie Simpson.

Lisa Marie Simpson

is a charismatic 8-year-old girl, who exceeds the standard achievement level of children her age. Not to everyone's surprise, she's also the moral center of her family. Lisa Simpson's a smart, witty, independent girl who focuses on her goals and strives to reach her potential, and at age 8 she's already a member of MENSA with an IQ of 159.

"Ned" Flanders

is the Simpson family's extremely religious next door neighbor and landlord. He is a genuinely well-meaning good-natured person and is one of the few in Springfield to whom that description applies. Homer often dislikes Flanders for his ways of doing things and his success.

Matt Groening

(Portland, 15 de febrero de 1954),​ registrado al nacer como Matthew Abraham Groening, es un dibujante, productor de televisión y escritor estadounidense, reconocido principalmente por ser el creador de Los Simpson. Es también el creador de Futurama y de la serie de Netflix (Des)encanto, y el autor de la tira cómica semanal La vida en el infierno, que publicaba en el semanario de Licorice Pizza, una tienda en la que trabajaba.
